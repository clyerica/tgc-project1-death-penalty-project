Singapore has executed over 50 people in the last 15 years

Although executions were halted during the course of the pandemic in 2019 and 2020, 10 new sentences were handed out and over 60 people await death row now.

Why Abolish the Death Penalty?

Singapore is one of the few countries in the world to have mandatory death sentences for drug trafficking. While supporters of capital punishment claim it acts as an effective deterrent, UN experts have said the death sentence is disproportionate for the amount of drugs in question. Many also say those convicted are victims of wider, underlying sociological issues.
